# Voice Models (Offline)

Yukti uses **offline, open-source speech recognition** via Vosk.

Models are **not bundled** due to size and licensing best practices.

## Supported Models
- vosk-model-small-en-us-0.15 (recommended)
- Other Vosk English models may work

## Installation
Download from:
https://alphacephei.com/vosk/models

Extract to:
models/vosk-model-*/

No audio or speech data ever leaves the device.
